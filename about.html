We started Cred because we were frustrated by the lack of in-depth resources around credit. This shouldn't be rocket science. Unfortunately, credit repair and debt consolidation companies spam the web with thinly veiled ads, selling you on the idea you need a "pro" or lawyer (here's our pro tip: you don't).

Getting insurance, buying a car, or renting an apartment? Your credit-worthiness can mean a difference of tens of thousands of dollars. Learn how to deal with collections, how to dispute errors on your credit report, and how to manage debt while protecting your credit score.

We share how we rebuilt our credit from 580 to 700 within a year.We are on a mission to empower each person to take their financial future into their own hands.
